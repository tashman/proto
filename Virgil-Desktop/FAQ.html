FAQ's

What is Virgil?

A simpler, easier smartphone experience
Virgil is a customizeable mobile interface designed to be easy and accessible to special needs individuals.
Virgil offers direct, pre-programmed options from a main screen, and takes the guess work out of navigating a smartphone.


Is it an app?

Virgil is a browser based web app, not a native mobile app.
Virgil will work on all major mobile browsers, regardless of phone type.


What kind of information do I enter?

Virgil lets you display important people, places, phone numbers, personal, and medical information.
You only have to input the information you are comfortable with.


Who is Virgil for?

Virgil was designed as an aid for special needs individuals who have a difficult time navigating smartphones, but can benefit from the services they provide.
As we designed Virgil, we expanded it to include the elderly, technologically wary, or anyone who finds smartphones confusng and just want to access a few core features.


How can Virgil help me?

Virgil presents a clear, unambiguous, easy to follow user interface for direct actions.
Smartphones can be challenging for everyone, even more so for those with limited capabilities.
Virgil bypasses the complex nature of the smartphone, simplifies the important functions, and presents clear, easy to read, one touch connection to important people, places, and information.


Will Virgil work on my phone?

If you have a smartphone (one with a current mobile web browser), Virgil will work on your phone.


Can I edit my settings on my phone?

Virgil is designed to be edited via desktop or tablet, and not by phone.
The phone is the 'run' environment.
For easiest setup and use, we recommend editing settings via desktop or tablet.


Can I run Virgil on my Desktop?

No. The desktop/tablet environment is meant for setup. We are a mobile service (for now.)


Why do I have to confirm everything?

Virgil is not a 'native' app, and cannot perform every action seamlessly (yet).
We appreciate your patience as we continue to improve our product.


Is there a Spanish version?

Virgil is not yet available in Spanish. See our más información page for more details. If you are interested in Virgil for another language, please send us an email and we will alert you as soon as it becomes available.
