What is Virgil?

 

Virgil makes your phone easier.

It's a simplified User Interface with customizeable options that allows you to display the features you want on your smartphone display.

No more smartphone sensory overload. We've designed Virgil for non-smartphone people.

Whether your user is a child, elderly, special needs, or just plain technologically challenged, Virgil can help make you and your' families lives simpler, easier and safer.
